Week 23 and 24 in Packit

June 16, 2025 · One min read
Laura Barcziová

Week 23-24 (June 3rd – June 16th)​

  • Team was attending and presenting at Flock to Fedora and Devconf.cz conferences. The highlight for us was connecting with so many Packit users in person! We want to thank everyone who attended our sessions and shared their feedback. If you are curious, here is the list of sessions we held:
    • Even more release automation talk
    • What about a better dist-git CI?
    • specfile library + norpm
    • Smarter package maintenance with Packit (workshop)
    • Kanban, our way
    • Breaking Changes Without Breaking Users: Lessons from Packit 1.0
    • Breaking Barriers: Cross-Distro CI/CD with Packit and OBS
  • Packit now exposes specfile_path from configuration (or its default value) as the environment variable PACKIT_SPECFILE_PATH for all Packit actions. (packit#2589)
